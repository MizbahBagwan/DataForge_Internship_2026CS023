# Project Report – Phase 9 (Python EDA Tables)

## Overview

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was performed using Pandas to summarize the cleaned dataset. Summary statistics, missing-value analysis, grouped tables, and ranking tables were created and exported as CSV files.

## Tables Created

* Numeric summary (`describe()`)
* Missing-value percentage table
* Sales by Category
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Order Year
* Quantity by Category
* Top 10 records by Sales
* Bottom 10 records by Sales

## Insights

1. The category with the highest total sales contributes the largest share of revenue.
2. Sales vary across regions, indicating geographic differences in business performance.
3. Yearly sales trends reveal changes in revenue over time.
4. The Top 10 sales records contribute significantly more revenue than the Bottom 10 records.
5. Missing values are minimal after the data cleaning process, improving the reliability of further analysis.
