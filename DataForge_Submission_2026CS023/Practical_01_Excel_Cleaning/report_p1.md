# Project Report - Phase 1

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains retail sales transactions with customer, product, sales, and shipping information. It can be used for business analysis, sales reporting, and profitability studies.

## Initial Observations

- Contains transactional sales records.
- Includes customer and product information.
- Includes geographic information.
- Contains sales, quantity, discount, and profit values.
- Suitable for sales trend and profitability analysis.

## Questions the Dataset Can Answer

1. Which product category generates the highest sales?
2. Which region has the highest total revenue?
3. Which customer segment is the most profitable?
4. How do discounts affect profit?
5. Which products are sold most frequently?
6. Which states or cities generate the highest sales?
7. What are the monthly sales trends?

## Conclusion

The dataset is suitable for cleaning, exploratory data analysis, visualization, and business intelligence reporting.
