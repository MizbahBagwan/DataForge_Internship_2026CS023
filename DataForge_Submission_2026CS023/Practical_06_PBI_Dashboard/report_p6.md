# Project Report – Phase 6 (Power BI Dashboard)

## Overview

A one-page interactive dashboard was developed in Power BI to summarize the Superstore dataset. The dashboard includes KPI cards, trend analysis, category comparisons, regional performance, and product rankings. Slicers allow users to filter the dashboard dynamically by time, category, and region.

## Dashboard Components

### KPI Cards

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Order Count
* Profit Margin

### Visualizations

* Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
* Sales by Category (Column Chart)
* Regional Performance (Bar Chart or Map)
* Top 10 Products by Sales (Bar Chart)

### Slicers

* Order Date
* Category
* Region

## Report Questions

### 1. What is the dashboard’s main business message?

The dashboard highlights overall sales performance, profitability, and regional trends, enabling decision-makers to quickly identify the best-performing categories, regions, and products while monitoring key business KPIs.

### 2. Which filter changes the story most strongly?

The Region filter has the greatest impact because sales and profit vary significantly across different regions. Applying this filter changes the rankings of categories and products, revealing regional performance differences.

### 3. What would you improve if you had one more day?

With additional time, I would improve the dashboard by adding drill-through pages, custom tooltips, conditional formatting for KPIs, and more advanced visualizations such as decomposition trees and forecasting to provide deeper business insights.
